Online Licensing System

The Regulatory Services Online Licensing System allows members of the public, applicants, and current licensees to search for license statuses, find licensees in their area, apply for new licenses, renew existing licenses, and pay fees online.

For a list of license types supported by this system, please see our Online Licensing Eligibility page.
